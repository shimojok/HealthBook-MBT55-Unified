# ⚡ 3-Stage Enzyme Cascade Mechanism

## Overview

The MBT55 3-stage cascade is a sequential metabolic relay where different functional groups dominate each phase, progressively transforming complex natural products into highly bioavailable active compounds.

## Stage 1: High-Speed Hydrolysis (0-6 hours)

**Conditions**: 38°C, aerobic, pH 6.0

**Key Players**: *Tateyama-Jodo (Bacillus sp.)*, amylase producers, protease producers, lipase producers

**Actions**:
- Starch → Oligosaccharides (amylase)
- Proteins → Peptides, amino acids (protease)
- Lipids → Free fatty acids (lipase)
- Cell wall disruption of raw herbs

**Example (Deer Velvet Antler)**:
IGF-1 and growth factors are liberated from the protein matrix by *Tateyama-Jodo* proteases.

## Stage 2: Metabolic Transformation (6-24 hours)

**Conditions**: 42°C, microaerophilic, pH 5.5

**Key Players**: *Tateyama-Menami (Atopostipes sp.)*, β-glucosidase producers, deglycosylation specialists

**Actions**:
- Glycoside → Aglycone (deglycosylation)
- Glycoprotein → Exposed sialic acid
- Flavonoid → Active aglycone form

**Deliberate Heat Stress**: The 42°C temperature intentionally stresses the consortium, boosting secondary metabolite (defense compound) production.

**Example (Coptis Berberine)**:
Berberine is demethylated and structurally modified for enhanced intestinal absorption.

## Stage 3: Deep Synthesis & Maturation (24-72 hours)

**Conditions**: 35°C, anaerobic, pH 6.5

**Key Players**: *Tateyama-Ryuo (Clostridium sp.)*, butyrate producers, mineral reducers

**Actions**:
- Anaerobic reduction of steroid skeletons
- Novel anti-inflammatory oxylipin synthesis
- Fulvic acid chelation of minerals
- Butyrate production via lactate cross-feeding

**Example (Earthworm × Deer Velvet × Artemisia)**:
The composite generates sialic acid-conjugated artemisinin (enhanced RBC targeting), new anti-inflammatory oxylipins, and cardioactive steroid metabolites—compounds not present in any single source material.
